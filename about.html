About Us

ELKARNAK established for the purpose of roasting /calcining mining materials such as limestone dolomite kaolin clay…etc

For the calcination of kaolin, the company has specified its plant located at ATAKA industrial zone, at Suez city, Egypt, which located very close to the main Egyptian ports on the gulf of Suez and red sea like Sokhna port and Adabya port, this is on one hand, on the other hand, the plant is two hours driving to Damietta port of Egypt on the med sea
