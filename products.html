Karnak Ref. 38/42

High-purity calcined kaolin ideal for refractory applications requiring low porosity and high thermal resistance.

Karnak Ref. 38/42 is a high-performance calcined kaolin engineered to meet the rigorous demands of refractory applications that require superior thermal resistance and minimal porosity. Its high purity and carefully balanced chemical composition make it an essential material for industries operating in extreme heat conditions.

Specifications

♦ Al2O3: ≥38%
♦ Fe2O3: ≤1.45%
♦ SiO2: ≤57%
♦ Refractoriness: ≥1730°C

Applications

♦ Refractory bricks.
♦ Glass fiber production.
